# Villain World Template

## Overview
A template for creating games where players take on the role of villains, with evil schemes, nefarious plans, and dark thematic elements.

## Core Requirements
1. Villain character development with dark progression systems
2. Evil schemes and world domination mechanics
3. Moral choice systems with negative consequences
4. Dark thematic environments and atmospheric storytelling

## Essential Systems

### Villain Progression
- Purpose: Enable character growth through dark powers and abilities
- Components: Corruption meter, dark skill trees, evil equipment
- Implementation: Data-driven approach with moral consequence tracking

### Scheme Management
- Purpose: Allow players to execute elaborate evil plans
- Components: Plot manager, resource allocator, success tracker
- Implementation: Event-driven system with branching scheme outcomes

## Assets Required

### Dark Assets
- Type: 3D Models and UI Elements
- Quantity: 100-200 gothic and sinister-themed assets
- Specifications: Dark color palettes with atmospheric lighting effects

## Best Practices
- Design meaningful evil choices with lasting consequences
- Create atmospheric environments that enhance dark themes
- Balance challenge to maintain tension without frustration

## Common Pitfalls
- Making villain progression too linear or predictable
- Poorly balanced moral choices lacking impact
